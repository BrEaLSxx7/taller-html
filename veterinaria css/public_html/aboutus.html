ABOUT US

CANO veterinary began in 2017, in the center of agro-industrial technologies (SENA). The medical team of the clinic, under the direction of Dr.CANO was always characterized by its permanent training, empathy, and vocation of service. Our goal is medical care based on prevention as well as the education of citizens who live with animals to achieve responsible tenure. La Clinica is a very pleasant place for those who visit us, with design, good management of the order and basic organization for good care. The safety cannons are very well studied, both in the waiting room, clinics, ray room and canine hairdressing to avoid the risks involved with working with animals. With a strong conviction that education is the basis for good health and a good quality of life, CANO veterinary is at the service of the community to work for this purpose.
