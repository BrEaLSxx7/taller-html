Fatty acids, DERMAPEL

Nutritional supplement with omega three and minerals highly bioavailable to improve the skin, appearance and shine of dogs and cats.

Provide Dermapel daily orally mixed with food in the following amounts:

Dogs weighing more than 25 kg: 30 g per day

Dogs between 10 and 25 kg of weight: 20 g daily

Dogs weighing less than 10 kg: 10 g per day

Cats up to 5 kg weight: 5 g per day

(One tablespoon is equal to 10 g).

The amount of supply can be modified according to the criteria of the Veterinarian Zootecnista or Zootecnista.
