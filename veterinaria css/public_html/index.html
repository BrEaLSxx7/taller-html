OBJECTIVE

Our goal is to provide the best we have at a human and professional level, dedicating our work to the prevention, diagnosis and treatment of diseases that are presented to our pets.

VETERINARY

Our products are designed and manufactured according to the needs and requirements of the market and the official control entities. The line consists of vaccines, vitamins, analgesics, antipyretics, anti-inflammatories, antiparasites, antibiotics, hormones, restorative tonics, disinfectants and others, which prevent diseases in cattle, horses, pigs, sheep, goats, cats, cats and rabbits.
